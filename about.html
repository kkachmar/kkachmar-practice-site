About Me

I am a double major in Graphic and Interactive Design and Computer Science at Quinnipiac University.

I began my education as a pre-med student at Northeastern University. However, I realized that my true passion lied in technology, and the ability to use design to make technological advancements better integrated into the lives of users. I have knowledge of computer science concepts, such as object-oriented design, data structures, and algorithm design, as well as knowledge of programming languages such as Java, Scala, Python, HTML, and CSS. In addition, I understand design thinking, am skilled in the Adobe Creative Suite, and understand the core concepts of UX/UI design and development.


In my free time I have been enjoying learning the basics of Javascript and web development, and hope to improve my skills as a designer and programmer.

I also love spending time with my new puppy, Lucky!
